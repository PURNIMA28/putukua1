About Banaras

Ghat

The scenic beauty of ghats during sumrise and sunset un-matched along side the calmness of river Ganga and seeing the people understanding the cycle of life and death

Food

Banarasi food has influence from all over India but still has an authentic taste. The Banarasi Pan, Kachori Sabji, Jalebi and Thandai are very famous.

People

The compasion and enthusiam shown by Banarasi people is uncomparable. They are so affectionate, warm, helpful and communicative that it won't be feel that you are away from home.

Gali

The Galies are inter-mingled like threads but serve as a network of commute from one place to other. The most famous Vishwanath Gali is like a serpant around the Vishwanath temple.

Artist

Banaras has been the incubation center for many artists for many decades, it is the birth place of art and artist both many prominent personalities like Ustad Bismilah khan, Sitara devi, Girja devi, Shambhu maharaj. It is still a craddle for many budding artists.

History

Banaras is older than history, It is said that it was one of the first cities during early civilization. The city has experienced rise and fall but has always been cente of interest of all rulers, spirtual entities and local people.

Spirituality

Banaras is a spiritual hub of all kinds of religion from Hindus, Muslim, Buddhist, and everuyone else have piligrimage sites here, one of the 12 jyotirlinga present are present here

Teaching

The Banaras Hindu University, Kashi Vidyapeeth, Sampurnand Sanskrit University, are Some of the rennowned educational institution which are known Internationaly

Handicraft

People of all ages love to posses handmade things and Banaras is one such place to find beautiful handloom sarees, handcrafted toys and other furnishings
